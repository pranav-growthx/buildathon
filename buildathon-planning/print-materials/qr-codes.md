## How to Create QR Codes

### Method 1: QR Code Generator (Recommended)

**Tool:** https://qr-code-generator.com

**Steps:**
1. Visit qr-code-generator.com
2. Select "URL" type
3. Paste your link
4. Customize (optional):
   - Add logo (Claude/buildathon logo)
   - Choose colors (keep high contrast for scanning)
5. Download as high-resolution PNG (300dpi minimum)
6. Test with your phone before printing

---

### Method 2: Google Chrome

**For quick QR codes:**

## Printing Guidelines

### Size Recommendations

**Large posters (GPT posters):**
- QR code: 4x4 inches minimum
- Total poster: 8.5x11" or 11x17"
- Scannable from 3-4 feet away

**Cheat sheet (handout):**
- QR code: 1x1 inch minimum
- Scannable from 1-2 feet away

### Print Quality

- **Resolution:** 300 DPI minimum
- **Color:** High contrast (black QR on white/bright background)
- **Paper:** Any, but bright colored paper helps visibility

## Troubleshooting

**QR code won't scan:**
- Increase size (4x4 inches minimum for distance scanning)
- Increase contrast (black on white works best)
- Reprint on non-glossy paper
- Ensure good lighting at venue

**Opens wrong link:**
- Regenerate QR code
- Double-check URL before generating
- Test again before reprinting

**Participants can't access GPT:**
- They need ChatGPT account (free is fine)
- GPT must be set to "Anyone with link" access
- Share backup: direct URL in chat
